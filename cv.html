CV

Education

2018 – 2019 MRes Psychology, Merit, The University of Manchester, UK
Conducted a pre-registered research study on age differences in impulsivity under joint supervision of Dr Ellen Poliakoff, Dr Jennifer McBride and Prof. Iracema Leroi. Responsible for task programming, as well as the recruitment of young and elderly adult participants, clinical assessment and data collection, creating analysis scripts in R, and overall lab management and data archiving.

Particular focus on research skills, data analysis and critical thinking. Consistently high results in statistics modules. Produced a first class grade dissertation, won 2nd place for the national Masters Award with the Psychology Postgraduate Affairs Group.
2014 – 2018 BSc (Hons) Cognitive Neuroscience and Psychology with Industrial Experience, Upper Second Class, The University of Manchester, UK
Final year project under Dr Ellen Poliakoff investigating the effects of age on inhibitory motor control with a novel physiological measure of continuous motor responding. Responsible for recruitment of young adult participants, data collection and analysis.

 Consistently high results in statistics and laboratory science modules. Presented two conference posters: a literature review on the project topic at the 7th International Conference Aspects of Neuroscience in Warsaw, Poland (2017), and a research poster at the Experimental Psychology Society meeting in Manchester, UK (2019).
